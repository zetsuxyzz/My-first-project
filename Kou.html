Kou Yamori (夜守コウ Yamori Kō)

Who is Kou Yamori?

He is a middle school student who began skipping classes to venture around his home city Komori at late night. Since he first began roaming around, Kou befriended Nazuna Nanakusa, a Vampire who he intended to develop romantic feelings for in order to be able to be turned into a vampire by her.

Nazuna and Kou Yamori's Relationship

His close time with Nazuna caught the attention of a group of vampires, led by Nico Hirata, who confirmed that Kou would be assassinated if he were not to become a vampire within a year. However, Kou and Nazuna succeeded in befriending each of them within a couple of months

Throughout his insomnia nights, he had came across more friends he had ever had before, and eventually, Kou encountered Anko Uguisu, a detective who sought to eliminate all vampires.

Kou and Nazuna grew closer to each other as friends, and they went up against Uguisu's campaign in order to save their allies.
