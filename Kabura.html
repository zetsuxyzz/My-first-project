Kabura Honda (本田カブラ Honda Kabura)

1. Who is Honda Kabura?

Kabura Honda (本ホン田ダカブラ Honda Kabura?) is a friend in Nico Hirata's clique who was originally a regular human patient at a hospital, where she fell in love with Haru Nanakusa (Mother Of Nazuna Nanakusa)

After Haru made Kabura her underling to cure her of her afflictions, she was requested by her to look after her child, Nazuna Nanakusa. Raising Nazuna as her own daughter, she continued to relish her life as a vampire and nurse at the hospital Haru worked at.

Following her first encounter with Nazuna's underling candidate, Kou Yamori , Kabura served an important role in uncovering Nazuna's past.

2. Her Fashion Style

Her outfits are defined by a preference for dresses and long skirts.

Her main outfit is a black, strapless bodycon dress with a turtleneck, a diaphanous mesh that covers a part of her chest and arms, and shoes with heels.

At work, she wears a classic nurse uniform that ends at her upper thighs, with slippers serving as her footwear. When she was still a human, her fashion style was about the same as her current one.

However, she only got to wear regular clothes when she was out of the hospital and mostly wore a standard patient gown.
